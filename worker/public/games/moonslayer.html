MOONSLAYER

Take the fewest souls. Or take them all.

The Moon grew fat on stolen hearts. Each midnight she deals her cursed cards and feeds on every soul careless hands let slip — her assassin the Black Queen (Q♠), worth thirteen in one cold stroke. You and your rivals Diana, Theo, and Vera hunt to carry the fewest souls. Reach 100 and the hunt ends; the lightest soul is crowned Moonslayer.

How to Play

  • Pass 3 cards to a neighbor, then follow the led suit if you can.
  • Highest card of the led suit wins the trick.
  • Each ♥ = 1 point. Q♠ = 13. Points are bad — low score wins.
  • Slay the Moon: take all 13 hearts + Q♠ and everyone else eats 26 instead.
